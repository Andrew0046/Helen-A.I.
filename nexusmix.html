Step 4 — Diagnostics

Review browser and hardware support. A warning does not always prevent launch; it means a feature may require manual fallback or may be unavailable in this browser.

Step 5 — Launch visor

The app requests fullscreen, attempts a landscape lock, opens the assigned camera by exact device ID, waits for the fullscreen viewport to stabilize, and then starts the two-eye renderer.

Place the phone in landscape

After launch, look downward to open the centered gaze menu. Hold the gaze marker over a control to activate it. Desktop fallback: press M to open the menu, move the mouse to aim, and click or dwell.
