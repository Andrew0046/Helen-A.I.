Step 5 — Launch visor

The app requests fullscreen, locks landscape, corrects Android screen-angle reporting, opens the assigned camera by exact device ID, and starts the two-eye camera platform.

Place the phone in landscape

After launch, the camera becomes the world around the platform. The Copilot node is anchored below your forward view like a physical object.
